# QRServe — Future Features (v4+)

> These are deliberately deferred. Do not build until v3 is stable and has paying clients.

---

## Booking System

- Calendar-based appointment booking for barbershops and salons
- Staff scheduling and availability windows
- Blocked dates management (already partially built)
- Booking confirmation emails
- Customer-facing booking page at `/book/:slug`
- Recurring appointment support

---

## Hotel Module

- Room management (check-in/check-out tracking)
- Guest request system (housekeeping, room service, maintenance)
- Multi-room QR codes
- Room status board for front desk

---

## White Label / Custom Domains

- Per-business custom domain support (`menu.myrestaurant.com`)
- Logo and accent color branding on scan/menu pages
- Remove QRServe branding for enterprise tier
- Custom email sender domain via Resend

---

## Mobile App

- React Native or PWA for staff dashboard
- Push notifications for new orders
- Offline menu browsing
- Camera-based QR scanner for staff

---

## Analytics

- Customer return rate
- Peak hour heatmap
- Menu item performance (views vs orders)
- Revenue forecasting
- Export to CSV / Google Sheets

## Payments at the Table

- Stripe Payment Intents on scan page
- Split bill support

- Digital receipts

## Multi-Location Management

- Location groups (e.g. all tables in the patio section)
